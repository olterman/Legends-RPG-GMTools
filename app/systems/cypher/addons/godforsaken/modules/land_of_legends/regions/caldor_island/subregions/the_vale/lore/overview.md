# The Vale Overview

The Vale is the sheltered heart of northwestern Caldor Island, cut off from the broader world by mountain ridges and protected as much by habit as by geography.

Its villages live by a pattern of river, lake, herd, ridge, and season. To outsiders, the Vale can seem peaceful, even timeless. To the people who live there, it is simply real: the mountains decide what can endure, and the water remembers what passes through.

The Vale is a strong home-base subregion because it supports:

- intimate village-scale stories
- layered local relationships
- slow discoveries about the wider island
- a believable contrast between familiar daily life and deeper hidden lore
