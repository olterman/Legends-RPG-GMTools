# Lilim Overview

Lilim should feel alluring, unsettling, and difficult to pin to the moral categories other peoples prefer.

They belong under `The Others` because they need room to remain themselves rather than being forced into a smaller, safer taxonomy. Their presence should complicate social space the moment they enter it.

Lilim lore works well with:

- charm edged with danger
- ambiguity as power
- intimacy, temptation, and unease
- the feeling of rules bending around presence
