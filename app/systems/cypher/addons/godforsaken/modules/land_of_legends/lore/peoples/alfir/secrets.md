# Alfir Secrets

Most peoples know the Alfir as ancient exiles. Far fewer understand what that really means.

- The Alfir did not simply migrate from a distant land. They fled a destroyed reality.
- Their arrival in this world was not harmless. The passage itself helped produce the scar through which Daelgast persists.
- The most refined Alfir traditions preserve fragments of pre-cataclysmic knowledge that could alter how the world understands history, magic, and cosmology.
- The Faltrim may still guard knowledge of deep fractures and gate-remnants unknown to the surface.
- The Duathrim are not merely estranged kin. They are a disciplined, covert power with long-range ambitions that extend beyond survival into silent rule.

For the Alfir themselves, the deepest secret may be emotional rather than strategic: immortality did not save them from becoming a people of refugees. It only made exile permanent.
