# Fenmir Highlands Overview

The Fenmir Highlands are a land of wind-scoured ridges, stone circles, ancestral memory, and restrained coexistence.

Here, Highland Fenmir and Highland Uruk live in a hard-earned balance shaped by custom, oath, and shared knowledge of harsh terrain. The highlands remain one of the clearest places where survival, spirituality, and memory still hold one another in tension.
