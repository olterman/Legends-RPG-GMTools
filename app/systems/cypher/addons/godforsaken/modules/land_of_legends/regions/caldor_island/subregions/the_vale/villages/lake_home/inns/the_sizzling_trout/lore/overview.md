# The Sizzling Trout Overview

The Sizzling Trout is Lake Home's warm social heart, where the day's catch turns into supper, gossip, and plans made over mugs and lamplight.

It is less guarded than a ridge tavern and more intimate than a harbor inn. People come here to hear what the lake is doing, who has arrived, what has gone missing, and what everyone is politely pretending not to worry about yet.

For the app and future generators, this kind of inn is a good example of a place that should hold both structural facts and expandable prose:

- proprietor and clientele in the manifest
- fuller local color, recurring scenes, and hooks in lore markdown
