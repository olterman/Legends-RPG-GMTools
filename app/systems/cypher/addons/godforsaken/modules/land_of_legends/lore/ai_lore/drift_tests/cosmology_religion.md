# Drift Test: Cosmology And Religion

Use this when a response starts drifting toward generic fantasy theology.

## Red Flags

- gods speak like clear personalities
- prayer is treated like a conversation hotline
- Daelgast is written as a demon lord
- the Breath of the World is treated as a friendly nature god

## Correction Move

Restate the topic in terms of pressure, resonance, condition, interpretation, misreading, and ritual alignment.
