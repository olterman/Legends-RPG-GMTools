# Duathrim Overview

The Duathrim are the shadow-bound Alfir branch: disciplined, hidden, and shaped by secrecy as a cultural principle rather than a temporary tactic.

They should feel like kin to the brighter Alfir branches, but in a way that sharpens the contrast rather than softens it. Their beauty is colder, their order more severe, and their relationship to truth more strategic.

Duathrim lore works best when it emphasizes:

- concealment as civilization
- hierarchy through function
- precision without sentimentality
- shadow, silence, and controlled revelation
