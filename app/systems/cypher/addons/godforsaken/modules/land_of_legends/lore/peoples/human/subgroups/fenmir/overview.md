# Fenmir Overview

Fenmir is the oldest deep-rooted human inheritance still recognizably alive in the setting.

It is not one stable nation. It is a shared ancestry carried through fracture, migration, spiritual law, and regional reinvention. Highlands, lowlands, wilds, and free cities all express Fenmir identity differently, but all remain shaped by mortality, ancestral memory, oath, and adaptation under pressure.

Fenmir culture should feel proud without security, martial without simplicity, and spiritually grounded without becoming passive. Their great strength is that they endure breakage without becoming formless.
