# Highland Uruk Overview

Highland Uruk belong to the harsh, wind-cut lands of the Fenmir Highlands and should feel shaped by that terrain in every aspect of their culture.

They are not simply the northern version of all Uruk. Highland coexistence, territorial custom, and upland survival produce a distinct identity, one that can sustain uneasy respect with Highland Fenmir and still remain fierce, proud, and dangerous.

Highland Uruk lore benefits from emphasis on:

- ridgeline territory
- personal honor and remembered slights
- spiritual or ancestral weight carried through harsh landscapes
- coexistence that is real, tense, and never sentimental
