# Human Overview

Humans are the most numerically widespread people in Land of Legends, but also the least unified.

They adapt, fracture, rebuild, and claim identity through region, survival, and political inheritance more often than through blood alone. In practice, a human from Caldor, a Fenmir coast-city, and a Xanthiri war-district may have less in common with each other than either has with some nonhuman neighbors.

As a top-level people group, `Human` is therefore mainly structural:

- it groups the major branches cleanly
- it supports search, browsing, and generation
- it should not flatten the major cultural differences between Caldor, Fenmir, and Xanthir
