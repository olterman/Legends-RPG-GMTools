# Cirdion Culture

Cirdion culture emphasizes dialogue, navigation, refinement, and exchange.

Kalaquendi society there treats art, science, magic, and craft as related disciplines. Shipwrights, diplomats, astronomers, and poets share intellectual ground. Education is universal by elite standards, and merit is meant to outweigh lineage or brute dominance.

This creates a culture that appears serene and open, but is also highly selective, self-regulating, and deeply aware of the burdens of long memory.
