# Galadhrim Overview

The Galadhrim are the forest-bound Alfir who chose covenant with the living world over city, empire, or overt guidance.

They dwell in grown settlements, shifting paths, spirit-bound groves, and wild domains where law is ecological rather than bureaucratic. Their magic is instinctive, conversational, and inseparable from plants, beasts, terrain, and the living memory of the forest itself.

Galadhrim should feel graceful without domesticity and wild without chaos. They are one of the setting's clearest expressions of beauty that can still kill without hesitation when the living world is threatened.
