# Lhainim Relationships

The Lhainim stand in a peculiar relation to nearly every other people.

Galadhrim treat them with reverence and caution because old pacts still matter. Fellic recognize them as elders in instinct and memory. Velim tolerate them because their role predates the ordered maintenance of gates and thresholds. Humans usually mistake their favor for luck and their wrath for superstition.

The Alfirin remain especially unsettling to them. The Lhainim read Alfirin permanence, brightness, and resistance to correction as symptoms of a later order imposed on a world that was once more responsive and alive.
