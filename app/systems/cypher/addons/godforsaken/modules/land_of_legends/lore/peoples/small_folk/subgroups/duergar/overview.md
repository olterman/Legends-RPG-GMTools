# Duergar Overview

Duergar belong under `Small Folk` in the rebuild, but they should never feel slight in weight, consequence, or cultural presence.

They are pressure-shaped, disciplined, and durable, carrying the sense that endurance is not only practical but moral. Their works should feel deliberate, engineered, and resistant to vanity.

Duergar content works especially well with:

- stone and forge imagery
- stoicism under strain
- responsibility over display
- communities built to endure weight
