# Human Culture

Human culture in the Lands of Legends is defined by mortality, adaptability, and scale.

Where elder peoples preserve memory across ages, humans compress transformation into generations. This makes them inconsistent, ambitious, inventive, and dangerous in ways immortals often underestimate.

The major human cultures express this differently:

- Caldorans pursue progress, trade, invention, and civic ambition
- Xanthiri channel faith, discipline, and sacrifice into militant order
- Fenmir peoples preserve honor, adaptability, ancestral law, and survival through regional variation
- the Free Cities turn pluralism, contract, and maritime opportunism into culture

Humans are often dismissed as brief-lived sparks. In practice, that brevity makes them the fastest civilization-scale force in the setting.
