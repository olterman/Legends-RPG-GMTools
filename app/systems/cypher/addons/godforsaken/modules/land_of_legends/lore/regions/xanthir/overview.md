# Xanthir Overview

Xanthir is a militant theocracy of blood, doctrine, and disciplined force.

To outsiders it is a land of zealots. To itself it is the last ordered truth in a faithless world. Its state, military, and theology are inseparable, and all of them are organized around the Red God as interpreted by the Crimson Synod.
