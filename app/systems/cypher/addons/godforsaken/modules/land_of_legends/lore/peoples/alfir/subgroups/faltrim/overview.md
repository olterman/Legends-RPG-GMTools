# Faltrim Overview

The Faltrim are the tide-born Alfir branch who refused to remain on the shore.

Where other Alfir built cities, forests, or hidden dominions, the Faltrim followed the great currents into the world below the waves. They live in migrating communities bound to leviathans, deep currents, resonance, and pressure, carrying memory through song rather than fixed record.

They are still unmistakably Alfir, but their culture treats motion as honesty and permanence as suspicion. That makes them one of the clearest surviving Alfir answers to exile: not to rebuild what was lost, but to keep moving until memory and world can breathe together again.
