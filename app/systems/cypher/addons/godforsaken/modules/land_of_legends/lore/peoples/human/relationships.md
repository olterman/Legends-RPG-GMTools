# Human Relationships

Humans stand in relationship to every other major people because they expand, adapt, and border almost everyone.

They trade with Kalaquendi, contest and emulate Alfir refinement, endure beside or against Uruk, misread Small Folk, fear and desire Lilim influence, and rely on inherited spaces and systems they did not create.

Even among themselves, their relationships are unstable:

- Caldor and Xanthir embody opposing civilizational instincts
- Fenmir identity names a cultural field rather than a unified nation
- Free Cities turn coexistence into policy without eliminating conflict

Humanity's relationship to the setting is therefore not one of ownership, but entanglement. Humans are everywhere because they cannot afford not to be.
