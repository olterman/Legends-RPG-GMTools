# Alfir Overview

The Alfir are one of the great elder peoples of Land of Legends: beautiful, dangerous, long-lived, and divided far more deeply than outsiders first understand.

To speak of the Alfir as one people is true only at the broadest level. Their shared origin matters, but just as important are the branches that grew apart from it. Kalaquendi, Galadhrim, Duathrim, and Faltrim all carry different relationships to light, memory, land, power, and separation.

For the rebuild, `Alfir` works best as a top-level parent identity:

- useful for browsing and grouping
- not specific enough to replace subgroup culture
- strong as a narrative umbrella for origin, kinship, and fracture

That means the richer day-to-day lore should usually live one level down, on the subgroup pages.
