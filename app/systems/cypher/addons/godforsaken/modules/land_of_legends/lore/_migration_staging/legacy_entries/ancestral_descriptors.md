# Ancestral descriptors
## Alfirin Cypher Descriptors
---
## **Kalaquendi (Sky Children)**
**You are a Sky-Touched Alfirin**
You were shaped by open horizons, high councils, and the endless curiosity of the heavens.
**Benefits**
+2 to Intellect Pool
Trained in persuasion, diplomacy, or social reasoning
Once per day, you can reroll a failed Intellect-based task involving knowledge, culture, or planning
**Inability**
Tasks involving secrecy, deception, or remaining unnoticed are hindered
**Flavor**
You instinctively seek understanding through dialogue and observation, even when silence would be wiser.
---
## **Galadhrim (Tree People)**
**You are a Wild-Bound Alfirin**
The forests recognize you as kin, and their old magic flows through your blood.
**Benefits**
+2 to Might Pool
Trained in survival, tracking, or nature-based magic
Once per day, you may reduce the difficulty of a task involving plants, animals, or natural terrain by one step
**Inability**
Tasks involving advanced technology or dense urban environments are hindered
**Flavor**
You react to danger as the forest does—instinctively, fiercely, and without apology.
---
## **Duathrim (Nightshades)**
**You are a Shadow-Forged Alfirin**
You were shaped by discipline, secrecy, and the silence between heartbeats.
**Benefits**
+2 to Speed Pool
Trained in stealth, assassination, or tactics
Once per day, you may ignore one step of difficulty when acting from concealment or surprise
**Inability**
Tasks involving open trust, empathy, or public leadership are hindered
**Flavor**
You move as though unseen even when standing in plain sight.
---
## **Falthrim (Children of the Deep)**
**You are a Tide-Born Alfirin**
You belong to the currents, not the shore.
**Benefits**
+2 to Might or Intellect Pool (choose when taking the descriptor)
Trained in swimming, navigation, or sound-based perception
You can breathe under water and suffer no penalties from underwater pressure or darkness
**Inability**
Tasks requiring prolonged time in dry, enclosed, or rigid environments are hindered
**Flavor**
You think in cycles, not moments, and hear truths others miss beneath surface noise.
---
# Optional Cross-Tribal Ability (GM Option)
**Immortal Perspective**
Once per session, an Alfirin may ask the GM one question about ancient history, long-term consequences, or repeating patterns. The answer is truthful but may be incomplete or symbolic.

## Uruk Cypher Descriptors
---
## **Highland Uruk**
**You are a Highland-Born Uruk**
You were shaped by cold winds, high valleys, and long nights of story and song.
Though outsiders call your people savage, your mind is sharpened by philosophy, oral history, and disciplined survival.
**Benefits**
+2 to Might Pool
Trained in endurance, climbing, or philosophical debate (your choice)
Once per day, you may ignore the effects of cold, fatigue, or hunger for one hour
**Inability**
Tasks involving refined court etiquette or dense bureaucratic systems are hindered
**Flavor**
You measure worth not by conquest, but by resolve and depth of thought.
---
## **Lowland Uruk**
**You are a Lowland Uruk**
You were forged in conflict, conquest, and unyielding tribal hierarchy.
Your people survive through dominance, fear, and relentless adaptation.
**Benefits**
+2 to Might or Speed Pool (choose when taking this descriptor)
Trained in intimidation, melee combat, or battlefield tactics
Once per day, you may deal +2 damage on a successful attack made while outnumbering or overpowering an enemy
**Inability**
Tasks involving subtlety, mercy, or long-term diplomacy are hindered
**Flavor**
You understand power as a language spoken through action, not promise.
---
## Optional Cultural Trait (GM Option)
**Uruk Presence**
Once per session, when an Uruk enters a scene, minor NPCs hesitate, react emotionally, or reassess their position—even if they do not flee outright.
---

**Benefits**

# Duergar & Lilim Cypher Descriptors
---
## **Duergar**
**You are a Forge-Bound Duergar**
You were shaped by stone, scarcity, and generations of deliberate endurance. Every act is weighed against memory and debt.
**Benefits**
+2 to Might Pool
Trained in endurance, stonecraft, or resisting poison and disease
You reduce all ambient environmental damage (heat, cold, pressure, toxins) by 1
**Inability**
Tasks involving speed, emotional openness, or improvisation are hindered
**Flavor**
You do not rush what must not fail.
---
### Optional Duergar Cultural Trait (GM Option)
**Ancestral Ledger**
Once per session, you may declare that a place, object, or foe is known to your people. Gain an asset on a related task—but the GM may later call in an ancestral obligation.
---
---
## **Lilim**
**You are a Wandering Lilim**
You walk the world in motion, art, and defiance, guided by an inner call no map can follow.
**Benefits**
+2 to Intellect or Speed Pool (choose when taking the descriptor)
Trained in arcane magic, performance, or social intuition
Once per day, you may turn emotional expression (song, dance, speech, art) into an asset on a magical or social task
**Inability**
Tasks requiring submission to rigid authority, doctrine, or routine are hindered
**Flavor**
Stillness is a kind of death.
---
### Optional Lilim Cultural Trait (GM Option)
**Inner Call**
Once per session, you may ask the GM what your instincts urge you toward in the current situation. Acting on it grants an asset—but ignoring it may cause inner dissonance or loss of focus.
---
## Tone Contrast (GM Guidance)
**Duergar** represent _weight, inheritance, and survival through restraint_
**Lilim** represent _motion, rebellion, and survival through expression_
They are philosophical opposites—both necessary, both dangerous in excess.
