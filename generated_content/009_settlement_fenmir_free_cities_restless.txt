Dark fantasy settlement prompt.

Setting:
Lands of Legends: A mythic fantasy world shaped by ancient migrations, damaged Gates, and conflicting origin myths. Tone and style: Epic, mystical, grounded, ancient-but-lived-in, high fantasy with mythic ambiguity. Cultures feel old, layered, and internally consistent. Core world truths: The Alfirin are immortal exiles who entered this world through Gates after a failed negotiation with the Velim, damaging reality. The Velim are ancient custodians of Gates and thresholds, forced into guardianship after the Alfirin passage went wrong. History is fragmented; myths contradict; no single culture is fully correct. Cultural themes: ancient wounds still shaping the present, survival over destiny, balance versus permanence, quiet guardianship versus visible power, myth over absolute truth.
