# Ered Engrin Relationships

Ered Engrin stands in quiet but consequential relationship to many powers.

Its strongest accord is with Almadir, where mountain and forest meet through balance rather than ownership. It maintains limited ties with surface realms, watches the Velim closely, and treats Daelgast not as myth but as containment problem.

The Duergar rarely explain themselves. Their relationships make more sense when one accepts that they are managing burdens other peoples barely perceive.
