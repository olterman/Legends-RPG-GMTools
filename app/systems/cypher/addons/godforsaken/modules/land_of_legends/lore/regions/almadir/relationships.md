# Almadir Relationships

Almadir relates cautiously to every neighboring power.

It respects Cirdion as kin but not as model. It distrusts Caldorite extraction. It opposes Xanthir openly. It remains close to Fellic and honors the Velim as watchers of balance.

Its relationship to the world is therefore protective rather than expansionist. Almadir is not trying to rule beyond itself. It is trying to prevent the world from forgetting what belonging feels like.
