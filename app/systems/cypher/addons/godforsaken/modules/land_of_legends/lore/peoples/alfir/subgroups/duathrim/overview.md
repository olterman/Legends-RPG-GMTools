# Duathrim Overview

The Duathrim are the most feared Alfir branch because they turned exile into discipline, secrecy, and long-term control.

They withdrew beyond common maps into Lomêanor and built a militarized, hierarchical order where every life is sorted by aptitude, trained for function, and embedded in cells that can operate invisibly for years. Magic, politics, and assassination are not separate arts to them. They are coordinated methods of shaping history without ever appearing to do so.

The Duathrim should feel beautiful, cold, strategic, and patient. They do not seek applause, only leverage.
