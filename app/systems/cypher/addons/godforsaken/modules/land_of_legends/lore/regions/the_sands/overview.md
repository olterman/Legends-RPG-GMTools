# The Sands Overview

The Sands are not simply desert. They are a visible scar where passage, corruption, and unfinished reality remain active in the land itself.

Ash dunes, black glass, poison water, wandering ruins, Gorothim, Gurthim, and heavy Daelgast presence make the region one of the clearest manifestations of world-damage in the setting.
