# Fenmir Free Cities Overview

The Fenmir Free Cities are the lowland human answer to survival after near-destruction.

Founded by Fenmir refugees who fled Uruk devastation, these coastal city-states turned desperation into lawless resilience. Freeport, Xul, and Kahn survive through sea-power, contract culture, mutual defense, privateering, and a willingness to let profit, exile, and necessity share the same harbor.

They are cosmopolitan by need rather than idealism. Foreigners, heretics, smugglers, escaped slaves, mercenaries, and merchants all find room there if they can live by the Free Accords and pay the cost of freedom. That makes the Free Cities feel vibrant, dangerous, and morally unstable in productive ways.
