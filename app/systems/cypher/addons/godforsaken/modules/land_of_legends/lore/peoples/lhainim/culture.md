# Lhainim Culture

Lhainim culture is older than settlement logic and older than moral systems built for mortal convenience.

They do not organize by nation, but by function, temperament, and the unfinished duties they inherited from older powers. Pixies, willows, pucks, nobs, redcaps, and the rest are not separate races so much as distinct expressions of one primordial kinship with the living world.

They aid, sabotage, bless, curse, protect, and mislead according to obligations older than law. This makes them feel unpredictable to outsiders, but not arbitrary. Lhainim are coherent inside a deeper pattern that most younger peoples no longer perceive.
