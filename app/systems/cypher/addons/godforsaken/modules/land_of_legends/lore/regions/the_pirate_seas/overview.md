# The Pirate Seas Overview

The Pirate Seas are the unstable maritime belt between Caldor Island, the Dragon's Teeth, the Bay of Skulls, and the Free Cities.

These waters are not just a travel route. They are a political condition defined by storms, hidden coves, wreck-strewn channels, privateering, and the constant negotiation between survival and opportunism. The Free Cities exert influence here, but never full control. Reputation, seamanship, and changing alliances matter as much as formal law.

This region works best when it feels like danger normalized into culture. People do not cross it because it is safe. They cross it because the world refuses to move without it.
