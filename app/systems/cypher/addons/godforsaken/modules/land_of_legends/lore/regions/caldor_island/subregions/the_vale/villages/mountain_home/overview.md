# Mountain Home Overview

Mountain Home is a ridge-watching village shaped by stone, herd paths, and caution.

People here live close to the slopes and closer still to old habits. Labor matters, reliability matters, and strangers are measured carefully before they are welcomed. The village is not hostile by nature, but it believes survival comes from attention, not optimism.

Mountain Home is a good anchor for stories about:

- practical tradition
- suspicious hospitality
- mining, trail work, and upland labor
- the quiet authority of those who understand the ridges
