# Freeport Overview

Freeport is the loudest promise the Free Cities make: fortune, refuge, danger, and reinvention, all packed into one harbor.

The city breathes through its docks, markets, taverns, and rival captains. It is a place where crews rebuild, merchants gamble, and every arrival is measured for what they might bring with them. The Great Lighthouse gives the harbor a shape; the people give it its appetite.

Freeport is especially useful as a generator and editor target because it naturally supports many content layers:

- inns and taverns
- captains and crews
- docks, markets, and factions
- rumors, jobs, betrayals, and hidden cargo
