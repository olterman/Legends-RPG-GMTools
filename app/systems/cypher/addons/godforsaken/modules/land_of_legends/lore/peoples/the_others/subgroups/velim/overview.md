# Velim Overview

Velim are part of `The Others` because they need conceptual room that the older small-folk grouping did not provide.

They should feel adjacent to the familiar world without being contained by it: recognizable enough to interact closely with other peoples, strange enough that those interactions never become entirely comfortable.

Velim stories are strongest when they lean into:

- near-familiar otherness
- social unease without simple monstrosity
- liminal belonging
- identities that expose the limits of older categories
