# Cirth Overview

Cirth is the oldest standing Alfirin city in the Lands of Legends and the first permanent settlement raised after their arrival through the Gate.

Built on white cliffs above a sheltered western bay, it functions as harbor, monument, and memory-site all at once. To the Kalaquendi, Cirth is proof that arrival was possible and that a new world could be engaged through openness, navigation, and inquiry rather than raw dominion.

Its terraces, sky-bridges, shipyards, and wind-catching towers all reinforce that identity. Cirth should feel like a city that still thinks of itself as a conversation between worlds.
