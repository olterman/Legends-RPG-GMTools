# Human Politics

Human politics is the most visibly unstable and dynamic political field in the setting.

Unlike the slower long-view governance of elder peoples, human polities rise, fracture, reform, and expand quickly. This produces empires, free cities, theocracies, clan networks, mercenary zones, and border societies in close proximity.

The main human political expressions include:

- Caldor's mercantile and intellectual empire-building
- Xanthir's disciplined theocratic state
- Fenmir's fractured regional identity without a single sovereign center
- the Free Cities' contract-based survival model

This makes humanity central to any campaign about change. Human politics is where doctrine, ambition, trade, fear, and opportunity collide in public.
