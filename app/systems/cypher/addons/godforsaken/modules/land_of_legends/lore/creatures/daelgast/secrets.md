# Daelgast Secrets

The central secret of Daelgast is that it persists not because it is unbeatable, but because the wound that let it in has never been healed.

It is compatible with unresolved passage. It thickens when reality is mishandled. It can be intensified by failed corrective measures, as shown by the Second Scar and the Pall of Tharun. Cults that treat it as revelation only worsen what they do not understand.

The setting becomes clearer once Daelgast is understood as a condition of unfinished reality rather than a villain with intent.
