# The Lucky Pick Overview

The Lucky Pick is the kind of inn that survives because it understands the village around it.

It is a place for miners, herders, trail wardens, and the occasional traveler who has earned a seat by making it this far. The mood leans sturdy rather than cheerful, but that steadiness is part of its comfort. News arrives here in fragments, one drink and one story at a time.

In play, The Lucky Pick is useful as:

- a rumor hub for ridge and trail trouble
- a meeting place for practical locals
- a grounded social center for Mountain Home
