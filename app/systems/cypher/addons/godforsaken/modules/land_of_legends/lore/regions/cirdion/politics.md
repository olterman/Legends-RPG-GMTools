# Cirdion Politics

Cirdion is governed by the High Council of Winds, a Kalaquendi meritocratic body shaped by insight, contribution, and long-term perspective.

Because its rulers are immortal, politics there moves slowly, often invisibly, and according to values outsiders find opaque. Stewardship, diplomacy, knowledge, and the advancement of craft are all political acts.

Cirdion sees itself as a bridge realm. That self-image is noble, useful, and potentially fragile.
