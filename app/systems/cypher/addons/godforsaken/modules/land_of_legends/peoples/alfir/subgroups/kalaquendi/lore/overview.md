# Kalaquendi Overview

The Kalaquendi are the high, luminous branch of the Alfir: sea-facing, patient, and closely associated with arrival, memory, and long-range vision.

They are the Alfir most likely to be mistaken for the whole of Alfir identity by outsiders, partly because their works are visible and partly because their self-possession reads as legitimacy. But they are only one branch, not the measure of all others.

Kalaquendi lore tends to work best when it emphasizes:

- elegance without softness
- patience without innocence
- light, sea, and horizon imagery
- achievement, knowledge, and long memory as cultural power
