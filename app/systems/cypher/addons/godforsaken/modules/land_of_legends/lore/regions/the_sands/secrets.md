# The Sands Secrets

The deepest truth of the Sands is that they are not merely corrupted land. They are unfinished land.

They became what they are because the Alfirin passage scarred reality there and Daelgast resonance took hold where closure failed. This makes the region less a cursed wilderness than an active symptom of cosmological damage.

If the Sands were ever to stabilize cleanly, it would likely mean something more dangerous than healing had occurred.
