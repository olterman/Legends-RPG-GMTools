# The Wounded History

Before this world became home, the Alfirin lived in a radiant First Homeland now lost beyond recovery. Its fall came in the Cataclysm of Unmaking: a total collapse of reality that drove the Alfirin into exile between worlds.

Their passage into the Lands of Legends was not clean. The Gates opened, but did not close as they should have. The breach calcified instead of healing, and Daelgast remained behind as a condition of corruption, erosion, and unfinished endings.

Everything that followed emerged from that wound:

- Alfirin refuge became division
- elder powers withdrew or hardened
- peoples adapted around fracture instead of harmony
- entire cultures learned to survive inside a world no longer fully in tune

Land of Legends is therefore a setting where ancient history is not distant. It remains active in geography, magic, memory, and the structure of every major people.
