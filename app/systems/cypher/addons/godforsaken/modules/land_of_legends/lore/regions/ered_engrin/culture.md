# Ered Engrin Culture

Duergar culture is organized around purpose, forge-clan, depth-circle, and the discipline of maintenance.

Art is reinforcement. Song calms pressure. Ritual stabilizes stone and what lies beneath it. Expansion is not a cultural virtue. Continuity is.

This makes Ered Engrin one of the setting's most structurally serious cultures: a people whose civilization is built around holding rather than claiming.
