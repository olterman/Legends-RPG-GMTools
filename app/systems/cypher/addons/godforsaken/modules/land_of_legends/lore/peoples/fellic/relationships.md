# Fellic Relationships

Fellic relationships are governed less by alliance than by recognition.

They maintain longstanding ties with Galadhrim and Velim because all three remain close to older patterns of balance. They are cautious with large civilizations, suspicious of expansionist projects, and alert to places where reality, instinct, or ecology have gone out of tune.

Other peoples tend to misread the Fellic as symbolic, quaint, or peripheral. In truth, they act as elders, observers, mediators, and living memory. Their relationship to the setting is less about control than about remembering when something fundamental has shifted too far.
