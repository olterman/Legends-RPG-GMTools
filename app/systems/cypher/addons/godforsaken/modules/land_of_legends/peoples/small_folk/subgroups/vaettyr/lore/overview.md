# Vaettyr Overview

Vaettyr should feel quick, local, and difficult to overawe: a people whose scale hides resilience rather than weakness.

They work best when their communities seem tightly bound to hidden labor, overlooked spaces, and social intelligence larger peoples routinely underestimate.

Vaettyr stories benefit from:

- compact but stubborn communities
- wit as protection
- domestic space with hidden depth
- underestimated competence
