# The Others Overview

`The Others` exists because some peoples do not fit cleanly into the older legacy taxonomy without distorting what they are.

This category is intentionally editorial. It gives Lilim and Velim a top-level home without forcing them into `Small Folk` or any other grouping that carries the wrong assumptions. That makes the category useful both structurally and conceptually: it preserves room for peoples who feel adjacent to the world's familiar lines without being contained by them.
