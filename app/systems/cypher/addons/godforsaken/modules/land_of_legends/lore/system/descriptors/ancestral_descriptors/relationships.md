# Descriptor Relationships

The descriptor packet reveals how the setting's peoples are meant to contrast at the table as much as in prose.

Several relationships stand out:

- Alfir descriptors emphasize branches of one exile people answering catastrophe in different ways.
- Uruk descriptors highlight the split between reflective endurance and militant domination.
- Human descriptors frame humanity as plural, adaptive, and driven by urgency rather than a single default culture.
- Fellic and Small Folk descriptors turn ancestry into role, pattern, and survival strategy instead of standard fantasy species templates.
- Lilim and Duergar form an especially strong contrast between motion and weight, expression and structure.

The value of this branch is that it lets those relationships remain legible in character creation, not only in deep lore reading.
