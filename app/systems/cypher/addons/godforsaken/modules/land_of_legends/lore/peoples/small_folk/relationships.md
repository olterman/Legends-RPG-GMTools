# Small Folk Relationships

The Small Folk relate to the world through leverage rather than dominance.

They are often ignored until they become indispensable. Gitz survive where larger powers fail. Vaettyr quietly inhabit the mechanical underside of civilization. Velim monitor thresholds others barely believe in. Duergar maintain burdens no one else can safely carry.

Their relationships with larger peoples are shaped by misreading:

- outsiders underestimate them
- elder peoples sometimes recognize their importance but not their autonomy
- empires treat them as resources, curiosities, or obstacles
- they often respond with caution, concealment, or highly selective alliance

Their true relationship is not primarily to crowns or nations. It is to systems: land, machine, threshold, balance, and continuity.
