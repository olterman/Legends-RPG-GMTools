# Caldor Overview

Caldor is the most ambitious human power of the age: a mercantile empire of innovation, trade, law, and civic ambition.

It looks forward where Xanthir looks backward. It believes the world can be improved, organized, and expanded through knowledge and commerce. That confidence has produced prosperity, influence, and dangerous blindness.
