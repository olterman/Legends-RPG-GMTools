# Fellic Culture

Fellic culture is old, local, and aspect-driven.

They do not define themselves through empire, migration, or centralized memory. They define themselves through lineage-as-aspect: beast forms that express role, temperament, inherited duty, and relationship to the wild.

Their cultures preserve:

- watching rather than ruling
- memory through embodied role
- settlement lightness and reversibility
- kinship with Galadhrim and recognition from the Velim

Each lineage expresses a different cultural emphasis, but together they form one of the clearest surviving expressions of the world before civilization made itself the measure of all things.
