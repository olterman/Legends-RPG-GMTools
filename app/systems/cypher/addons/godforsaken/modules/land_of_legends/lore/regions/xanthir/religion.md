# Xanthir Religion

Xanthir's religious life is built around the Red God as struggle, sacrifice, purification, and divine trial.

Faith there is not private devotion. It is the organizing structure of law, army, art, status, and punishment. This makes the region essential to the setting's theology because it demonstrates how a real force of resistance can be weaponized into conquest and certainty.

Xanthir is dangerous not because it believes in nothing, but because it believes too rigidly in one reading of pressure.
