# Daelgast Overview

Daelgast is not a people, god, or demon. It is a condition of corruption that clung to the world through the failed Alfirin passage.

It spreads where closure fails: where death does not release, where magic stagnates, where time hesitates, and where reality remains wounded. It does not plan. It does not judge. It spoils.
