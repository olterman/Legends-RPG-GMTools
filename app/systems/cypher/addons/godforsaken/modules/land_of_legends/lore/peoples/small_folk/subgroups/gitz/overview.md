# Gitz Overview

The Gitz are not feral leftovers. They are one of the most adaptive and cunning peoples in the setting.

They thrive in the Fenmir Wilds and other hard-to-govern spaces where larger civilizations struggle to maintain order. Their clans organize around specialized skills such as traps, herbal alchemy, animal handling, stealth warfare, and improvised diplomacy, and they survive by treating wit as a higher form of strength than brute force.

The world's prejudice against them matters. Larger peoples often mistake them for degenerate scavengers or failed Uruk kin, which means Gitz stories work best when that underestimation becomes a tactical error.
