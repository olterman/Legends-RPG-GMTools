# Fenmir Free Cities Overview

The Fenmir Free Cities are where the coast stops pretending law is simple.

Freeport, Xul, and Kahn survive by negotiation, leverage, and the understanding that commerce and violence are never very far apart. The Free Accords keep the cities from devouring each other outright, but they do not make them safe. They make them workable.

This region is ideal for stories about:

- pirate politics
- mercantile intrigue
- mixed crews and mixed loyalties
- contracts, betrayals, and temporary alliances
