# Gorthim Overview

The Gorthim are the horrors born where corruption, pressure, and damaged reality produce something more extreme than simple undeath.

If the Gurthim are the dead that failed to finish dying, the Gorthim are forms that should never have cohered at all. They belong in wastelands, breach-zones, and places where Daelgast saturation has moved beyond haunting into active deformation.

They should feel like manifestations of a world held in the wrong shape.
