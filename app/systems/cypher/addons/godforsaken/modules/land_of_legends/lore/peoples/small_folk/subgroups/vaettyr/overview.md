# Vaettyr Overview

The Vaettyr are subterranean tinker-folk whose curiosity is as defining as their size.

They live in burrows, hidden vaults, and forgotten tunnels beneath the visible world, building cultures around workshops, apprenticeships, and careful experimentation rather than crowns or temples. Their devices blend engineering and esoteric insight, and their oldest myths frame them as repairers awakened by deep fractures in the world.

Vaettyr stories should feel ingenious, infrastructural, and slightly unnerving. They improve systems quietly, understand pressures others never notice, and often know more about what holds the world together than any surface scholar.
