# Regions Overview

The regions of the Lands of Legends are not just locations. They are the visible expression of history, wound, culture, and pressure.

Some lands preserve the memory of arrival, like Cirdion. Some preserve older rhythms, like Almadir and Ered Engrin. Some are fractured mortal heartlands, like Fenmir. Some are projects of ideology, like Xanthir and Caldor. Some are not properly lands at all anymore, like the Sands.

Across the module, geography carries meaning:

- coasts and harbors hold trade, passage, and openness
- mountains hold memory, endurance, and containment
- forests hold covenant, instinct, and resistance to domination
- islands and valleys preserve isolation and local continuity
- wastelands hold unresolved damage

This means places are never just scenery. They are arguments made physical.
