# Land of Legends Overview

The Lands of Legends is a mythic fantasy world shaped by ancient passage, fracture, and restraint.

Reality was wounded when the immortal Alfirin fled a shattered homeland and passed imperfectly through the Gates guarded by the Velim. That failed passage scarred existence and allowed Daelgast, a non-sentient cosmic dissonance from beyond the world, to seep into creation. Since then, beauty, wonder, and survival have existed beside corruption, distortion, and unresolved consequence.

This is not a world defined by neat moral absolutes or destiny-driven prophecy. It is a world of pressures:

- the wound of Daelgast
- the resisting fire of the Red God
- the motion and refusal embodied by Lilith and the Lilim
- the immediacy of the Breath of the World carried by the living

Its peoples endure not because the world is safe, but because they have learned to survive through memory, pact, adaptation, and restraint.
