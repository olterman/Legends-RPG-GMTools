# Daelgast Relationships

Daelgast defines itself through pressure on other forces rather than through intent of its own.

The Velim relate to it as load, containment, and proof that some passages should never be stabilized. The Alfir relate to it with silence and guilt, because its persistence is entangled with their survival. The Old Gods are weakened where dissonance spreads. Lilith and the Red God oppose it in different ways, one through motion and refusal, the other through resistance and sacrifice.

Mortals encounter Daelgast through symptoms: undead, tainted zones, impossible fatigue in magic, and places where beauty refuses to resolve properly. It becomes most dangerous when belief systems, cults, or policies begin organizing themselves around its continued presence.
