# Drift Test: Xanthir Doctrine

Use this when Xanthir starts reading like generic evil-cleric fantasy.

## Red Flags

- Xanthir is written as cartoon villain religion
- doctrine loses its internal tension
- the Red God is treated as a simple speaking tyrant
- every Xanthiri answer sounds identical

## Correction Move

Bring back doctrinal structure, coercive order, schism, militarized theology, and the gap between Xanthiri belief and deeper cosmology.
