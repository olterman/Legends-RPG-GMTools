# Lowland Uruk Overview

Lowland Uruk are the expansionist, feared, and brutally pragmatic face of Uruk power.

Their societies are organized around conquest, dominance, militarized hierarchy, and the open claim that strength is law. They maintain war-chiefs, stratified labor, and conquest cultures that have earned the Uruk their most monstrous reputation in the settled world.

That reputation should remain true without flattening them into caricature. Lowland Uruk carry old laws, submerged traditions, and prophecies of unity or ruin beneath their public violence, which makes them more dangerous, not less.
