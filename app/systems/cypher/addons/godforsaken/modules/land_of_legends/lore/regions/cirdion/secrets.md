# Cirdion Secrets

Beneath the public halls of Cirdion lies the Hall of Unclosed Charts, where maps, routes, and destinations remain deliberately unfinished.

This is not merely scholarship. It is an admission that the Kalaquendi know the first arrival left unresolved consequences. Some routes should not be retraced. Some truths should not yet be made public. And some among Cirdion still wonder whether what was damaged can ever be mended without unacceptable cost.
