# Daelgast History

Daelgast entered the world with the failed Alfirin passage, but it did not become fully legible to later peoples until its consequences accumulated.

At first it was the residue of an unresolved wound: corruption pooling where death failed, magic stagnated, and endings could not complete. More recently, the attempted corrective anchoring at Cael-Tharun created what Velim now call the Second Scar. Instead of easing the damage, that ritual reconnected the world briefly to the interstitial aether and caused Daelgast to thicken around a fresh rupture.

This matters because Daelgast is not merely old damage lingering in the background. It is a continuing historical pressure that worsens when the world is mishandled.
