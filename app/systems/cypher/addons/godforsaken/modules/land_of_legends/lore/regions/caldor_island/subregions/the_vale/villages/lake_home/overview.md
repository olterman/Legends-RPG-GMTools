# Lake Home Overview

Lake Home is the softer edge of the Vale: a fishing village where daily life follows the moods of the water.

Where Mountain Home looks to the ridges, Lake Home looks to the shoreline, the catch, and the weather crossing the surface of the lake. It is still a Vale village, still shaped by isolation and old custom, but its rhythm is gentler and more open to exchange.

Lake Home works well for stories of:

- local trade and seasonal routine
- water-bound superstition
- quiet community tensions
- outsiders who arrive by necessity rather than conquest
