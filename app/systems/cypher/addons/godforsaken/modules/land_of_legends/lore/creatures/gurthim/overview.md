# Gurthim Overview

The Gurthim are the restless dead: undead manifestations produced when death fails to resolve cleanly in a wounded world.

They are not a race with a homeland or culture in the ordinary sense. They are symptoms of mismatch, places where memory, flesh, and ending no longer separate as they should. That makes them part of the Daelgast ecology even when they are not directed by any will.

They should feel tragic, wrong, and persistent rather than theatrical.
