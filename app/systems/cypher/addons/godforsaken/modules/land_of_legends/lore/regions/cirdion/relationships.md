# Cirdion Relationships

Cirdion stands in relationship to nearly every major power because it is both old and outward-facing.

It trades with Caldor and the Free Cities, watches Xanthir with distrust, honors but does not fully align with the Galadhrim, fears the Duathrim in private, and remembers the Falthrim through absence and song.

Its relationships are shaped by a core tension: Cirdion wants to remain the realm that keeps dialogue possible, while knowing that dialogue alone cannot resolve every wound.
