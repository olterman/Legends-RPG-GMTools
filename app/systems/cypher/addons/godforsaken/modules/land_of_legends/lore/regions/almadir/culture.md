# Almadir Culture

Galadhrim culture in Almadir rejects central city logic in favor of grove, season, omen, and living arrangement.

Homes are grown, not built. Leadership is situational. The land is not treated as property, but as covenant. Magic is instinctive, responsive, and resistant to abstraction.

This makes Almadir one of the clearest counterweights in the setting to fixed empire, linear progress, and architectural permanence.
