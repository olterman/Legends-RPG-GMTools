# Ancestral Descriptors

The ancestral descriptors define how Land of Legends peoples express themselves in Cypher-facing play terms.

They are not just mechanical modifiers. They are compressed statements of worldview, pressure, and inheritance. A Highland Uruk descriptor should feel different from a Lowland Uruk one for the same reason their cultures diverge in lore. A Lilim descriptor should carry motion, intuition, and refusal, while a Duergar one should carry weight, debt, and endurance.

The legacy descriptor set currently covers:

- Alfirin lineages
- Highland and Lowland Uruk
- major human cultures
- Fellic aspects
- Small Folk groups
- Lhainim
- Duergar and Lilim

This branch is the canonical landing place for that material as we split it into cleaner setting-aware system references.
