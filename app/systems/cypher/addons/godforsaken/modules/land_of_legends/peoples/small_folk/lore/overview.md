# Small Folk Overview

`Small Folk` is a useful structural category, but not a claim that its branches are interchangeable.

Vaettyr, Duergar, and Gitz do not become one culture simply because other peoples group them together by stature. The value of the parent category is navigational and thematic: it gathers peoples who often occupy overlooked spaces, hidden economies, tight communities, and survival niches others dismiss until too late.

The rich lore should still live primarily at subgroup level, where their differences can stay sharp.
