# Alfir History

Alfir history begins before the Lands of Legends.

In the First Homeland, the Alfir lived in a realm of radiant order where matter, magic, and thought were not sharply divided. That world ended in the Cataclysm of Unmaking, a collapse so complete that every surviving memory is shaded by grief, contradiction, and myth.

The Great Exodus followed. Using world-sailing vessels and aether-bound craft, the surviving Alfir fled between realities. Some ships were lost. Some emerged altered. Those who endured became a people defined not by triumph, but by survival after the impossible.

When they arrived in this world, they laid the first foundations of what would become Cird and treated the new land as refuge rather than conquest. But refuge did not restore unity. The First Sundering broke the Alfir into diverging ways of life:

- the Kalaquendi turned toward sky, sea, trade, and diplomacy
- the Galadhrim bound themselves to forest and living wilderness
- the Faltrim followed the deeps and the great sea-beasts
- the Duathrim disappeared into secrecy, hierarchy, and long designs

The history of the Alfir is therefore the history of exile becoming plurality.
