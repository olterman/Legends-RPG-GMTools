# The Others Culture

The Others gathers peoples whose place in the world is not well described by ordinary settlement logic.

At present this branch is dominated by two very different inheritances:

- the Lilim, who embody motion, performance, rebellion, and itinerant identity
- the Velim, whose existence is bound to thresholds, gates, and the maintenance of reality

Bringing them together does not imply sameness. It acknowledges that both stand outside most ordinary political and social categories. They are peoples defined less by territory than by principle, task, or way of being.

The Others is therefore a useful rebuild category for lore that concerns ontological outsiders, moving peoples, and necessary presences that do not fit neat civilizational boxes.
