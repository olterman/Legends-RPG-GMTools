# Ered Engrin Secrets

There are sealed depths beneath Ered Engrin where sound, stone, and metal no longer behave properly.

Duergar tradition does not frame these places as mysteries to solve. They are warnings to obey. The deeper secret is that containment there may be tied directly to the same world-damage that produced Daelgast elsewhere.

What the Duergar hold shut is not simply geological.
