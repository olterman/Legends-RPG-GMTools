# Almadir Overview

Almadir is the living forest-realm of the Galadhrim, a place not ruled so much as tended.

To outsiders it appears as a vast ancient wood. In truth it is a living mosaic of groves, migrating paths, sacred places, and regions the forest itself keeps beyond ordinary use. Almadir is a realm of participation rather than ownership.
