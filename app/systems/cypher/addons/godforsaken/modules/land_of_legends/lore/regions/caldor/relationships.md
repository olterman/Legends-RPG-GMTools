# Caldor Relationships

Caldor relates to the rest of the setting through trade, interpretation, and expansion rather than naked conquest alone.

It encourages exchange with peoples who can enrich commerce or knowledge, studies Alfir works with hungry admiration, competes with Xanthir on both ideological and geopolitical fronts, and pushes influence outward through roads, fleets, treaties, and institutions. To some cultures that reads as enlightenment. To others it reads as polished encroachment.

Caldor is strongest in the setting when it is neither villainized nor idealized. It is a productive civilization whose confidence makes it transformative and difficult to resist.
