# Xanthir Overview

Xanthiri humanity is shaped by the Red God's theocracy, where faith, law, labor, and warfare have fused into one political-religious machine.

This culture should feel severe, disciplined, and convinced rather than merely cruel. In Xanthir, conquest is doctrine, hierarchy is sacred, and survival is treated as evidence of divine favor. Priesthood and military power are inseparable, and even ordinary life becomes ritualized service to the god-state.

Xanthir works best when it feels frighteningly coherent. It is not chaos in red colors. It is order sharpened into a weapon.
