# Fellic Overview

The Fellic are a broad beast-kin people grouping whose visible diversity is part of their identity rather than an exception to it.

Feline, Lupine, Canine, Ursine, Bovine, Chelonian, Avian, and Anatine lines create a wide spectrum of body, temperament, and cultural emphasis. That means `Fellic` is best understood as a high-level ancestry umbrella, not a finished cultural statement.

For content generation, this makes Fellic especially useful:

- top-level identity can drive common ancestry and broad assumptions
- subgroup lore can shape local look, temperament, customs, and conflict
