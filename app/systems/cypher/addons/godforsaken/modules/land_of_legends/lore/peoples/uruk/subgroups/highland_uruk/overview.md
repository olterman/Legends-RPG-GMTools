# Highland Uruk Overview

Highland Uruk are the philosophical and upland-hardened face of Uruk civilization.

They live in the severe plateaus and valleys of the Fenmir Highlands, where survival demands endurance, memory, and thought as much as force. Their uneasy peace with the Highland Fenmir is built on reciprocal respect between peoples who understand thin air, hard land, oath, and betrayal.

Highland Uruk should never read as "civilized exceptions" to other Uruk. They are fully Uruk, but shaped by oral philosophy, epic song, ancestral routes, and the conviction that strength without thought is incomplete.
