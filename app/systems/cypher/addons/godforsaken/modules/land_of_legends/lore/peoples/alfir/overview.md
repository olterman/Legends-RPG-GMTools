# Alfir Overview

The Alfir are the Immortal Ones, the first of the elder peoples to set foot upon the Lands of Legends.

They are not natives of this world. In their deepest memory lies a First Homeland beyond this reality, a realm of radiant order and living light that was destroyed in the Cataclysm of Unmaking. Faced with extinction, they fled through the dimensional aether and arrived here as exiles, survivors, and unwilling bearers of consequence.

Their arrival did not create unity. Survival fractured them. What began as one people became many branches, each carrying the weight of exile differently:

- the Kalaquendi turned outward toward sea, sky, and exchange
- the Galadhrim rooted themselves in living wilderness
- the Duathrim embraced secrecy, discipline, and shadow
- the Faltrim passed beyond the shore into the deep

To speak of the Alfir as a single people is therefore true only in blood and origin. In temperament, culture, and relationship to the world, they are already a history of division.
