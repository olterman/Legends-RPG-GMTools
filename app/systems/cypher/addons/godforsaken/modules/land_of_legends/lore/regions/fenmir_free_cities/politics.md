# Fenmir Free Cities Politics

Politics in the Free Cities is negotiated rather than inherited.

No single throne unifies them. Power flows through captains, harbor interests, merchant houses, privateer networks, councils, and the remembered need for mutual defense against forces larger than any one city. Contracts matter, but so do grudges, tides, and reputations that can rise or drown in a season.

The Free Accords are what keep the cities from collapsing into constant self-destruction. They do not create virtue. They create enough shared structure for rivalry, profit, and survival to coexist without tearing the coast apart.
