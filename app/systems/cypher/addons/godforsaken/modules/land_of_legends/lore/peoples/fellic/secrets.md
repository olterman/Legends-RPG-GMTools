# Fellic Secrets

The Fellic carry a form of knowledge the rest of the setting often underestimates.

Their stories imply that they remember the world from before the Alfirin passage, before stable kingdoms, and before later peoples mistook civilization for the only meaningful shape of life. They speak obliquely about the bright wound, the narrow door, and the pressure that changed the wild into something harder and less patient.

That memory is not fully shared. It is distributed across aspects, sayings, ritual roles, and old cautions. Even their child-verses carry warnings about the land screaming, the sky shining, the Velim humming, and the Bear sitting.

The deepest Fellic secret is not a hidden conspiracy. It is that they may understand the setting's oldest transitions better than the peoples who claim the loudest histories.
