# Cirth Politics

Cirth is governed by the High Council of Winds, a meritocratic body drawn from scholars, navigators, artisans, and envoys.

Merit here means contribution to understanding rather than pedigree or tenure. New routes, cultural insight, diplomatic breakthroughs, and innovations in magic or craft all matter more than inherited right. Because the Kalaquendi are immortal, office can last for centuries or be surrendered the moment a councilor believes their insight has gone stale.

This makes Cirth seem patient, elitist, and opaque to outsiders. It also means the city continues to frame politics as stewardship of arrival, memory, and contact rather than as simple territorial administration.
