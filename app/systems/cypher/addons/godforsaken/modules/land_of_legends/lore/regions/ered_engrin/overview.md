# Ered Engrin Overview

Ered Engrin is the iron mountain realm of the Duergar, a place of vertical holds, stone burden, and containment.

It is not a frontier to be conquered, but a depth to be held. Duergar civilization beneath the mountains exists to endure pressure and prevent certain failures from reaching the surface.
