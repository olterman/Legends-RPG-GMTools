# Xanthir Wilds Overview

The Xanthir Wilds are the frontier that proves Xanthir does not fully master the world it claims to discipline.

Wetlands, ruined waterways, hidden enclaves, dangerous ecologies, and monster-haunted margins survive under or beside the reach of the theocracy. Some communities endure as tolerated vassals, others as concealed heretics, escaped peoples, or forgotten lineages that have learned to live where imperial control thins.

This region should feel different from central Xanthir. It is still shaped by the Red God's empire, but there the doctrine frays against terrain, spirits, practical magic, and the endurance of people who were never fully absorbed.
