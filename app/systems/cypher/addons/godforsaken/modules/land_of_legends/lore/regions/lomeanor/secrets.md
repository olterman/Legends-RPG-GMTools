# Lomeanor Secrets

Lomeanor's greatest strength is not military secrecy alone. It is the fact that many surrounding powers still treat it as rumor, myth, or deniable influence.

The deeper secret is that the Duath are not fully unified in their relationship to Daelgast. Some seek containment. Some seek leverage. That internal divide could shape the future of the north more than any external invasion.
