# Caldoran Overview

Caldorans are the imperial human branch most shaped by civic ambition, trade, law, and the belief that order can be built if enough effort and structure are applied.

They should feel capable, cultured, and expansive, but never neutral. Their virtues are entangled with pressure, expectation, and the assumption that progress justifies reach.

Caldoran content is strongest when it leans into:

- law and commerce
- civic pride
- layered faith and public culture
- ambition that can inspire or suffocate
