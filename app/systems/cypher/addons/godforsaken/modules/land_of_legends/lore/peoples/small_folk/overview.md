# Small Folk Overview

The peoples collectively known as the Small Folk are often dismissed by larger races as minor, harmless, or quaint. That mistake has proven costly more than once.

Though diminutive in stature, the Small Folk are ancient, clever, and deeply entangled with the hidden workings of the world. They thrive in places others overlook: deep forests, forgotten tunnels, broken machines, hidden thresholds, and the seams between realities.

In the rebuild taxonomy, this group includes:

- Vaettyr
- Duergar
- Gitz

They do not share a single culture, but they do share a structural truth: larger powers routinely underestimate them, and the world continues to function in part because they are still there doing what others fail to notice.
