# Xanthir Politics

Xanthir politics is theology rendered into command structure.

The Crimson Synod governs through doctrine, law, war, and ritual authority. Civil and military offices overlap by design, and public life is arranged through hierarchy, obedience, and sacrificial meaning.

Its greatest political weakness is also one of its strengths: internal doctrinal difference cannot be openly admitted for long without becoming crisis.
