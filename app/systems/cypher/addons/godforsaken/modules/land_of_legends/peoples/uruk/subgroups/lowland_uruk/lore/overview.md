# Lowland Uruk Overview

Lowland Uruk are shaped by contested plains, broken borders, and the harsher realities of expansion, survival, and repeated conflict.

Compared to their highland kin, they should feel less rooted in mutual coexistence and more defined by pressure, incursion, and practical force. That does not make them simpler, only differently hardened.

Lowland Uruk lore is strongest when it emphasizes:

- mobility and aggression under pressure
- fractured frontier identity
- survival through force and adaptation
- memory carried through conquest and loss
