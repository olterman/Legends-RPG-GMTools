# Caldoran Overview

The Caldorans are the most expansive and self-confident human culture in the Lands of Legends.

Their identity is anchored in cities, trade networks, workshops, roads, canals, scholarship, and the belief that improvement is not only possible but morally necessary. Caldorans are builders of systems: civic, mercantile, intellectual, and imperial.

At their best, they feel like a human renaissance in motion. Art, engineering, commerce, natural philosophy, and political ambition all reinforce one another. At their worst, that same confidence becomes cultural pressure, soft domination, and the assumption that history should reorganize itself around their progress.
