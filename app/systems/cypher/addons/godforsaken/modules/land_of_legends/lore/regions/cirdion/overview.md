# Cirdion Overview

Cirdion is the first Alfir realm made permanent after the arrival from beyond the world. It is not only a nation, but the place where arrival, refuge, and curiosity became structure.

Built along western coasts, cliffs, bays, and the great city of Cird, Cirdion preserves the Kalaquendi answer to exile: understand the world, navigate it, and remain open enough to survive inside it.

Its public face is elegance, trade, scholarship, and patient governance. Its unspoken depth is the knowledge that arrival carried consequences no council has fully resolved.
