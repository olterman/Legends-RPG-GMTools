# AI Lore: Gorthim

## Recognition Profile

- Creature category tied to horror, corruption, and warped manifestation rather than a playable people.
- Look for body distortion, fear pressure, anti-natural presence, and wrongness rather than coherent culture.

## Allowed Identity Labels

- gorthim
- horror
- horrors
- corrupted_manifestation

## Disambiguation Rules

- Treat Gorthim as creature categories, not peoples or lineages.
- Do not write them as civilized monster-nations unless a very specific local case demands it.
- Keep them closer to manifestation, corruption, and existential pressure than to stock fantasy beastfolk.

## Working Guidance

This document exists because the older AI layer was careful about creature identity drift. Gorthim should read as horrors first, not as “another race with spikes.”
