# Xanthir Overview

Xanthiri humans are shaped by militant doctrine, ritual discipline, and a political imagination that prefers certainty to mercy.

They should not feel merely cruel; they should feel convinced. Their culture is strongest when it presents harsh order as virtue and expansion as sacred necessity.

Xanthiri lore works especially well with:

- red-banner severity
- disciplined hierarchy
- doctrine as daily life
- faith turned into state force
