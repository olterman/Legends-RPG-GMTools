# Galadhrim Overview

The Galadhrim are the forest-bound branch of the Alfir, shaped less by the open horizon and more by living depth, instinct, and ancient ecological presence.

Where the Kalaquendi often feel like light made architectural, the Galadhrim feel like memory rooted in growth, predation, and covenant. They should feel beautiful, but never tame; graceful, but never merely ornamental.

Galadhrim content works especially well when it leans into:

- feral elegance
- forest intelligence
- pact-bound life with the land
- slowness, instinct, and danger intertwined
