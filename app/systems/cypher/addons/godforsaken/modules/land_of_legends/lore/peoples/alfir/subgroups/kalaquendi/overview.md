# Kalaquendi Overview

The Kalaquendi are the most visible and widely recognized branch of the Alfir.

They built the cliff-cities, shipyards, harbors, and councils that many outsiders mistake for Alfir civilization as a whole. Their culture privileges sea-lanes, light, scholarship, diplomacy, navigation, and achievement measured across centuries rather than seasons.

But the Kalaquendi should not read as uncomplicated benefactors. Their openness is real, yet it carries elitism, opacity, and the assumption that patient intelligence earns authority. They are the branch most committed to engaging the world, and therefore the branch most likely to shape it quietly in their own image.
