# Fenmir Overview

Fenmir is less a nation than a condition: mortal, fractured, stubborn, and still unwilling to disappear.

To call someone Fenmir is to place them inside a history of breakage, migration, survival, ancestral memory, and regional reinvention. Highlands, lowlands, wilds, and free cities all express that inheritance differently, but the shared thread is recognizably human and recognizably Fenmir.

Fenmir stories are especially strong when they involve:

- pride without stable empire
- oath, memory, and local custom
- improvisation under pressure
- the tension between kinship and regional divergence
