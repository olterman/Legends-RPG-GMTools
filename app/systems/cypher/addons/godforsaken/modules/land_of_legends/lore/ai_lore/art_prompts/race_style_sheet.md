# AI Art Prompt Style Sheet

## Negative Prompt

photorealism, anime, manga, chibi, flat colors, sketchy lines, heavy outlines, harsh shadows, muddy palette, overexposed lighting, plastic skin, AI artifacts

## Compact Style

Clean polished illustration, stylized realism, minimal crisp linework, painterly digital color, expressive faces, believable anatomy, soft even lighting, rich balanced saturation, clear material textures, readable silhouettes, modern graphic-novel / tabletop RPG finish

## Race Prompt Seeds

- Duergar: Stocky dense humanoids, stone- or ash-toned skin, severe faces, heavy utilitarian armor, dark metals and stone, subterranean forge or cavern setting.
- Vaettyr: Short sturdy subterranean humanoids, large hands and eyes, work-worn clothing, goggles and tools, surrounded by ancient machinery and stone.
- Gitz: Small agile humanoids, wiry builds, sharp features, bright eyes, scavenged clothing and tools, clever wary expressions, forest-dwelling survivors.
- Fellic: Humanoid animal-aspected beings, natural fur and traits, minimal natural clothing, expressive posture, wilderness harmony.
- Lilim: Graceful humanoids with infernal or alien traits, horns or unusual eyes, ritual adornments, flowing eclectic clothing, subtle magical glow, intense emotional presence.
- Uruk: Large powerfully built humanoids, thick musculature, pronounced lower tusks, skin from pale blue-white to deep green, tribal clothing, grounded dominant stance.
- Velim: Tiny luminous humanoids, semi-translucent light-forms, simplified features, hovering or light-footed, glowing motes, subtle spatial distortion.
- Alfir: Tall refined humanoids, subtly pointed ears, luminous eyes, slender elegant builds, cool-toned skin, living wood, light metal, crystal, composed posture.
